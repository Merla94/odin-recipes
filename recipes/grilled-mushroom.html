GRILLED MUSHROOM

Image source Freepik

This is a juicy stuffed mushroom recipe that can be grilled as well as baked in the oven, however we will only be covering the grilling bit of it. It is a good recipes as a starter for a party.

    INGREDIENTS

  • 500g mushrooms
  • 1 bellpepper of your choice
  • cheddar cheese to cover
  • 1 garlic clove

    STEPS

  1. remove all stalks from mushroooms
  2. chop bellpepper and garlic cloves fine
  3. Grate/slice the cheese

    4. optional:

    You may fry chopped bell peper ad garlic cloves to make them soft but it is not needed.

  4. Fill the mushrooms with the bellpepper/garlic mix
  5. Cover the top/filled part of mushroom with cheese
  6. Put mushrooms on the grill for 5 minutes or until juice is seeping out
  7. Plate and ENJOY!
