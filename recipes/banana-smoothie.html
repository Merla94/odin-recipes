Banana Smoothie

Up close image of a banana smoothie with whole and cut bananas alongside it.

image source Image by freepik

Ingredients

  • 2 Bananas
  • 1 Cup almond milk
  • 1 tbs Peanut Butter
  • 1 tsp Honey
  • cinamon powder

Steps

  1. Peel and cut Bananas
  2. Add Bananas, almond milk, Peanut Butter and Honey to Blender
  3. Blend until smooth consistency
  4. Pour contents of Blender to glass and top off with Cinamon powder
  5. Enjoy!
