Lemon Pasta

    Ingredients

  • 1 whole lemon
  • 500g Penna pasta
  • 250g fresh cream
  • Parcely
  • 25g Butter

    Steps

  1. Add pasta to already boiling water and let it boil till it is at a firmness of your liking
  2. Melt butter in a frying pan
  3. Add pasta and freshly squeezed lemon to the frying pan on low heat
  4. Add fresh cream on Medium heat for 5 minutes and then let simmer for 2 minutes
  5. Plate pasta, garnish with Parcely and ENJOY!
